Introduction

The first statement of this book – we are technologically ready to step into the age of intelligent freedom. But before I start to explain the key characteristics of the age, please read a small introduction for this book just below.

These are major intentions for writing this book:

  1. To share with others my knowledge collected and deduced by me yet.
  2. To represent UABRO.
  3. To represent myself.
  4. To improve myself continuously working on my thoughts and their expression.

Here you will read lots of concepts and ideas, reasons and results based on both funny and not at all stories. Treat my words in most general, fundamental way and remember there is no specific audience of my readers – people of any age, race, wealth and from any location of the world are welcome.
