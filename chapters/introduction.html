Introduction

The first statement of this book – we are technologically ready to step into the age of intelligent freedom. But before I start to explain the key characteristics of the age, please read a small introduction to this book just below.

These are my major intentions for writing the book:

  • To explain and represent UABRO.
  • To share with others my knowledge and vision.
  • To represent me.
  • To improve my thoughts and their expression.

Treat my words in most general, fundamental way and remember there is no specific audience of my readers – people of any age, race, wealth and from any location of the world are welcome.
