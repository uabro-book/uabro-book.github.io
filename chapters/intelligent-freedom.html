Intelligent freedom

Intelligent freedom is a state of society where all of its members spend their life wisely in accordance with their conscious will.

Nowadays people are entitled with lots of freedoms like freedom of speech, of thought, of expression. In turn, intelligent freedom introduces actual ability to disclose power of personal intelligence.

New paragraph. Questions like these:

What our real power? What is important for individual, for groups of people and all humanity at all?

What is value?

What we should change in our behavior to be more dignified and honored? What the purpose of any activity?

There are few simple rules of intelligent freedom: 1) Physical work, especially accurate and/or heavy, should be done by robots. No humans. 2) Work, that can be described by an algorithm, should be done by robots. No humans. 3) All other stuff are exclusively under jurisdiction of humans. No robots.

We should always remember that humans are on top of organisms hierarchy in the whole galaxy. Thus we have biggest responsibility for the world's appearance and how it operates.
