Through the history, we can see two main periods. First, period ofdifferent forms of totalitarianism - where some people (monarchs, slave owners) fully or mostly control others (slaves, population). Second – democracy – where each human has its own freedom of thought, speech, movement, choice etc.

UABRO is a very general, fundamental and sophisticated system thus it has noparticular, specific and simple idea. However, within UABRO we can create countless number of divisions and activities, particular projects each of which will have one simple task – regeneration of human neuron cells, for instance.

The point is to govern and direct all projects and activities in the sameway, in accordance with general principles. Because this allows us to achieve all goals together in the most efficient way.
